# Compliance

The Slices platform implements a multi-layered compliance system to ensure regulatory compliance and participant protection.

## Multi-Signature Approval

Every RWA token and pool deployment requires approval from authorized signers. This provides:

- **Identity verification** — signers verify the business owner's identity
- **Due diligence** — signers review the business before approving
- **Fraud prevention** — multiple independent approvals reduce the risk of malicious deployments

The approval process works as follows:

1. Business requests approval signatures via the platform
2. Authorized signers review the request
3. Each signer independently signs the deployment parameters
4. The deployment transaction includes all signatures for on-chain verification

## AI Risk Evaluation

Before deployment, each business undergoes an AI-powered risk evaluation:

- **Stage 1** — initial analysis of the business description
- **Stage 2** — deeper analysis incorporating documents and images
- **Risk score** — a numerical score (0-100) indicating the assessed risk level
- **Factors** — detailed breakdown of evaluation factors with impact ratings

The evaluation considers the business description, supporting documents, and uploaded images.

## Platform Controls

The protocol includes several safety mechanisms:

- **Emergency pause** — Governance can pause RWA contracts and pools in case of emergency. Note: unpause differs by contract type — RWA contracts require **Timelock** to unpause, while Pools can be unpaused directly by **Governance**.
- **Fee controls** — all fee parameters are bounded by Governance-configured ranges
- **Parameter validation** — all pool parameters are validated against Config limits at deployment
- **Timelock** — critical operations (treasury withdrawals, contract unpausing) are delayed

## Participant Responsibilities

Participants should:

- Review the business profile and risk score before contributing
- Understand that tokenized assets carry inherent risks
- Monitor pool performance through the platform's charts and portfolio tools
- Be aware that returns depend on the business fulfilling its obligations
