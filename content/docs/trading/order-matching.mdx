# Order Matching

Unlike traditional order book exchanges, Slices uses an Automated Market Maker (AMM) for all trading. There is no order matching — every trade executes instantly against the pool's reserves.

## How the AMM Works

The AMM uses the constant product formula:

```
k = virtualHoldReserve × virtualRwaReserve
```

When you buy RWA tokens, the pool's virtual RWA reserve decreases and the virtual HOLD reserve increases (plus the real HOLD reserve). The constant `k` ensures that the product of the two virtual reserves always remains the same.

### Worked Example: Buy

If `virtualHoldReserve = 200,000`, `virtualRwaReserve = 100,000`, and you buy 1,000 RWA:

```
newVirtualRwaReserve = 100,000 - 1,000 = 99,000
newVirtualHoldReserve = k / 99,000 = 200,000 × 100,000 / 99,000 ≈ 202,020
holdAmount = 202,020 - 200,000 = 2,020 HOLD
```

So buying 1,000 RWA tokens costs 2,020 HOLD at this price level.

## Price Impact

The `priceImpactPercent` parameter determines how much the price moves per trade. Combined with the `liquidityCoefficient`, it controls the depth of the virtual reserves. Higher liquidity coefficients mean larger virtual reserves, which dampen price movements for the same trade size.

## Slippage Protection

Both `mint()` and `burn()` include slippage protection parameters:

- **`maxHoldAmount`** — the maximum HOLD you're willing to spend (for minting)
- **`minHoldAmount`** — the minimum HOLD you're willing to receive (for burning)
- **`validUntil`** — a deadline timestamp to prevent stale transactions

If the actual price moves beyond your tolerance, the transaction reverts.

## Fees

Trading fees are applied to both mint and burn operations:

- **Entry fee** — a percentage of the HOLD amount paid when minting (configured in basis points)
- **Exit fee** — a percentage of the HOLD amount received when burning (configured in basis points)

Fees are sent to the Treasury contract and are controlled by Governance.

## Real-Time Updates

You can subscribe to real-time trading events via WebSocket:

- **[`priceUpdates(poolAddress)`](/docs/api/charts/price-updates)** — notified when the pool's price changes
- **[`transactionUpdates(poolAddress)`](/docs/api/charts/transaction-updates)** — notified when a mint or burn occurs

Each event includes the pool address, timestamp, and relevant state changes.
