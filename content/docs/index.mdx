# Slices Documentation

Slices is a platform for tokenizing real-world assets (RWAs) and launching crowdfunding pools on BSC (Binance Smart Chain). Businesses raise capital from participants who contribute HOLD tokens and receive RWA tokens in return. An on-chain AMM provides price discovery, tranches automate distributions, and a DAO governs protocol parameters.

## How It Works

```
Business → AI Evaluation → Multi-sig Approval →
RWA Contract Deployed → Pool Created → Pool Deployed →
Participants Buy RWA → Target Reached → Tranches Distributed → Returns
```

1. A business is created on the platform
2. The AI evaluator analyzes the business and assigns a risk score (0-100)
3. Authorized signers review and approve the RWA token deployment
4. `Factory.deployRWA()` deploys an ERC1155 token contract for the business
5. The business configures a pool — funding target, RWA supply, fees, tranches, AMM parameters
6. Authorized signers approve the pool deployment
7. `Factory.deployPool()` deploys the pool contract linked to the RWA token
8. Participants contribute HOLD tokens and receive RWA tokens via the pool's AMM
9. When the funding target is reached, tranches automate fund distribution to the business
10. The business returns funds via incoming tranches, participants burn RWA tokens to claim returns + bonus
