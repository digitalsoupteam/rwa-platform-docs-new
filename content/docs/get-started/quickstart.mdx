# Quick Start

This guide walks you through launching your first RWA pool end-to-end. Each step links to the detailed documentation and API reference — follow along to go from zero to a live pool.

## Prerequisites

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
- HOLD tokens for pool creation fees
- BNB for gas on BSC Testnet (get from the [Testnet Faucet](/docs/testnet-faucet/overview))

## Step 1: Create a Company

A company is the organizational entity that owns businesses and pools. [Create a company](/docs/api/company/company/create-company) via GraphQL with a name, description, country, and social links.

Once created, the [AI evaluator](/docs/ai-evaluator/overview) can analyze your business and assign a risk score.

## Step 2: Create a Business

Under your company, [create a business](/docs/api/rwa/businesses/create-business) — the tokenizable entity. You can use [AI-assisted creation](/docs/api/rwa/businesses/create-business-with-ai) to generate metadata from a description, or set fields manually:

- **Name** — your business name
- **Business type** — `growth`, `startup`, or `franchise` (see [Asset Types](/docs/tokenization/asset-types))
- **Country** — jurisdiction for compliance
- **Description** — what your business does
- **Tags** — categorization labels

## Step 3: Deploy the RWA Token Contract

After your business is created, request multi-signature approval for RWA deployment:

1. [Request approval signatures](/docs/api/rwa/businesses/request-business-approval-signatures) — authorized signers review and sign off-chain
2. [Check the signature task](/docs/api/signers-manager/get-signature-task) — poll until `completed = true`
3. Call `Factory.deployRWA()` on-chain with the collected signatures

See [Token Creation](/docs/tokenization/token-creation) for the full process and contract details.

## Step 4: Configure and Deploy a Pool

With your RWA token deployed, configure your pool:

1. [Create the pool](/docs/api/rwa/pools/create-pool) — link it to your business
2. [Edit the pool](/docs/api/rwa/pools/edit-pool) — set funding target, RWA supply, fees, periods, tranches, AMM parameters
3. [Request pool approval signatures](/docs/api/rwa/pools/request-pool-approval-signatures) — signers review and sign
4. Call `Factory.deployPool()` on-chain with the collected signatures + fee in HOLD

For a detailed walkthrough of pool configuration and deployment, see [Launch a Pool](/docs/pools/launch-pool).

## Step 5: Promote and Collect

Once your pool is live:

1. Share the pool link with potential participants
2. Track contributions in real-time via [charts](/docs/charts/overview) and [portfolio](/docs/api/portfolio/get-balances)
3. When the target is reached, claim outgoing tranches via `Pool.claimOutgoingTranches()`
4. Return funds via `Pool.returnIncomingTranche()` to fulfill your obligations
